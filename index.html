EXPLORING THE WORLD



Water is an essential natural resource that sustains all life on Earth, existing in solid, liquid, and gaseous forms. It covers over 70% of the planet's surface and is critical for human survival, used for drinking, agriculture, and hygiene, while also playing a vital role in the environment by supporting ecosystems, transportation, and weather patterns. Protecting water through conservation and preventing pollution is crucial, as freshwater is a limited and precious resource.
